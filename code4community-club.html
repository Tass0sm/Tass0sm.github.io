Code4Community Club

  • In Progress - October 16, 2021
  • Purpose: Working with a group to make an instructional program.

Summary

I joined the OSU Code4Community club at the beginning of this semester. I am part of a team working on a platforming game meant for teaching middle schoolers basic programming skills. The president of the club also pulled me aside recently and asked me to help lead the group. I'm excited to help out, and I might even try to officially join the club's leadership. I haven't had this much fun working on a project in a while.

Lessons Learned

  • The Entity-Component-System design pattern for games.
  • The Phaser 3 game engine.
