Summary

After seeing news about Alphafold2, I wanted to try playing with it and maybe using it to predict the structure of a protein. Unfortunately, the Alphafold2 code was not available at the time, so I chose to play with ProSPr. I eventually completed that structure prediction after some troubleshooting, and I contributed a small fix to the project along the way.

  • How to use docker.
