Retroactive Recorder

  • Completed - April 14, 2021
  • Purpose: Make something my friends could use.

Summary

A tool that buffers the last n seconds of audio and writes them to a disk on command in raw PCM form. Made very minimally. The only dependency is JACK.

Lessons Learned

  • Features of the ogg container format.
  • Features of the FLAC encoding format.
